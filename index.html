About

I am a highly motivated researcher specializing in Natural Language Processing (NLP) and AI, passionate about advancing research in fact verification of health information using AI, information extraction using large language and vision-language models and AI for document understanding. I thrive on solving real-world problems with cutting-edge technology and contributing to the open-source community.
