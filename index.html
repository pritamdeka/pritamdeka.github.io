About

I'm an AI Engineer and Research Fellow at Queen's University Belfast, specialising in practical AI systems across LLMs, RAG, agentic AI, multimodal AI and document intelligence. I turn research ideas into working systems, demos and reusable pipelines. I also co-founded Khyontek AI, building SME-focused AI products.
